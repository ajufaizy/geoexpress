About Us

Geo Express Logistics is one of the best international shipping companies in United Arab Emirates that provides peace of mind for its international clients and unparalleled customer service.

Our highly-trained local teams in each of our locations develop specific, comprehensive solutions for each step of the shipping process to relieve the bureaucratic burden from our clients’ shoulders. This includes shipment, customs clearance, delivery of goods to the customer’s warehouse, and shipment updates.

When it comes to global markets and the continually shifting climate of regulations and political upheaval that govern or affect international shipping, Geo Express Logistics agents possess the expertise and experience to deliver your goods to international markets efficiently. We are your strategic partner in global shipping and documentation and are continually seeking to enhance our logistics services to cater to your needs.

Simply put, we are the clear choice for top-quality services. In a largely technology driven field of work, Geo Express Logistics takes a people-oriented approach to international importing and exporting services.
